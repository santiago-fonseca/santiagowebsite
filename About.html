About me...

Howdy! My name is Santiago Fonseca, and I'm a current freshman at Texas A&M, class of 2024. For starters, I come from a Latino household, where both of my parents are immigrants, coming directly from Guadalajara, Mexico to come and raise my siblings and I here in America. I was born in Murfreesboro, Tennessee where I spent the first 5 years of my life until we eventually settled down in Texas once my father found a stable source of income. I have two siblings, an older brother who recently graduatedfrom A&M this year with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering (class of 2020), and a younger sister. Spanish was my first language, where English quickly came sometime after, and due to neither of my parents having received anything higher than an elementary level education, I spent a lot of my time studying and practicing the material myself since I wasn't able to rely on my parents as a crutch academically. On my free time I enjoy playing videogames with my buddies, hanging out, and watching the latest shows on Hulu.

I have current plans to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering here at Texas A&M, with my primary objective being to gain an in depth understanding in the field and to secure internships to gain experience throughout college. The science behind designing and building circuits, computers, and all sorts of electrical technology has always been something that has interested me, though I only began to want to pursue it in college my senior year of high school in Physics C, specifically in the E&M portion of the course. Overall I'm excited to study Electrical Engineering here at Texas A&M and Gig'em!
